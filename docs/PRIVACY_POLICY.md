# Privacy Policy — Wond

**Last updated:** July 2026

## Overview

Wond is a minimalist white noise app for sleep, focus, and relaxation. We designed it to work without collecting personal data.

## Data Collection

We do **not** collect, sell, or share personal information. The app has:

- No user accounts
- No analytics SDKs
- No third-party tracking or advertising

## Local Storage

The following data is stored **only on your device**:

- Favorite sounds and mixes
- Custom mixes (SwiftData)
- Settings such as default sleep timer (UserDefaults)

This data is not transmitted to our servers because we do not operate backend services for Wond.

## Audio Content

Bundled sounds are sourced from [BigSoundBank](https://bigsoundbank.com) under CC0 (public domain). White, brown, and pink noise are generated locally in the app.

## Children's Privacy

Wond does not knowingly collect information from children. The app is suitable for general audiences.
